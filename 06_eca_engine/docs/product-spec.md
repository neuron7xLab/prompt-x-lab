# Product Specification — Cognitive Engine v1.0

## Product category
Executive Cognitive Operating System / Cognitive Decision Infrastructure Layer

## Intended buyers
- founders
- chief of staff
- heads of research
- boutique consultancies
- product strategy teams
- performance and learning design teams

## Core promise
Improve reasoning quality, reduce empty output, enforce operational structure, and preserve mechanistic rigor when human performance and system design intersect.

## Product boundaries
This product supports:
- decision framing
- strategic analysis
- human-performance protocols
- system design
- implementation planning
- reasoning audits

This product does not replace:
- legal review
- licensed medical advice
- regulated domain compliance
- empirical experimentation

## Deployment models
1. Internal deployment
2. Tenant-isolated enterprise deployment
3. Licensed advisory workflow deployment

## Versioning
- Alpha
- Stable
- Enterprise
- Custom

## Success metrics
- improved coherence over baseline
- improved operational density over baseline
- lower logical leap rate
- high format compliance
- high implementation readiness
